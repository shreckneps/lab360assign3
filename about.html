My name is Spencer Butler. I am a third-year student at the University of Idaho. My major is computer science. Currently, I am taking classes for Software Engineering and Databases, in addition to a couple other non-CS classes. This website was created for the databases class. For the software engineering class, we are creating videogames in groups, in order to facilitate the learning of techniques appropriate for working together. My group is making a game where the player controls a bee. What fun! Bees are quite interesting creatures. Bees are not plants. They fly around with their wings and sometimes sting things with their stingers. This is an appropriate use of a stinger. They like to land on plants with flowers. Why? Who knows! That's the mystery of bees: where do they come from, why do they do what they do, and who's going to stop them? More research ought be done, but scientists are afraid. As they should be.
