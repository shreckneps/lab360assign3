This site makes no use of cookies. There is no reason for this site to use cookies. Perhaps, if there were actual logins being created, cookies would be used to store authentication tokens, and reduce how often a user needs to sign in. There are no actual logins though. As such, this site does not use cookies. You do not have to consent to the use of cookies here, as there is no use of cookies here. You cannot configure which cookies you would like to be used. Again, there are no cookies here. Cookies do exist in real life though. There are a great variety of cookies that exist. Some examples, ranked loosely by how much I like them:

  1. Chocolate Chip
  2. Sugar
  3. Gingersnaps
  4. Snickerdoodles
  5. Oatmeal Chocolate Chip
  6. Raisin
  7. Oatmeal
There are a variety of recipes for all of these types of cookies. If you seek to try any of them, you are free to Google recipes. You also can simply purchase these cookies from a store, though the quality is generally worse.
