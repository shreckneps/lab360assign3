This site does not use or store your data for any purpose. All information you enter is discarded and all forms are purely for aesthetic purposes. Information is not collected or used for tracking purposes. Information is not provided to advertising partners. There are no advertisers with whom I have partnered. However, you still should not enter sensitive information into any of the forms on this website. It is bad practice to provide such data to unknown and sketchy websites, even if they claim (truthfully here) that they do not make use of the data. You ought be aware of your own digital hygiene, and share your data only with sites and services which have genuine purposes for using it.
